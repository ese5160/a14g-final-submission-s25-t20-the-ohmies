Hardware Requirements

Team 20 "The Ohmies"

HRS 01 – SAMD21 Microcontroller

Status: Achieved

  • Verified hardware components in BOM and final assembly.

HRS 02 – MyoWare EMG Sensor

Status: Partial

  • Measured analog output signal using oscilloscope to confirm functionality.
  • Readings were wildly inaccurate, requiring software compensation.

HRS 03 – LSM6DSOX IMU Sensors

Status: Achieved

  • Verified I2C communication using logic analyzer and sensor data reading.

HRS 04 – MG90S Servo Motors

Status: Achieved

  • Physical inspection and testing of motor response for all four fingers.

HRS 05 – 3.7V Li-ion Battery

Status: Achieved

  • Load-testing and measured input/output voltages on test points.

HRS 06 – 3.3V Voltage Regulation

Status: Achieved

  • Measured voltage output from regulator circuit for MCU and sensors.

HRS 07 – 5.0V Boost Converter

Status: Partial

  • Measured voltage output at servo power pins.
  • On-board boost converter failed, requiring external power solution.

HRS 08 – Minimum Grip Strength (20 lbs)

Status: Partial

  • Measured grip force using force gauge.
  • Achieved 16 lbs of force, below target specification.

HRS 09 – Individual Servo Control

Status: Achieved

  • Verified independent actuation of each finger through testing.

HRS 10 – Emergency Stop Button

Status: Achieved

  • Functionality verified through repeated emergency stop tests.
  • All motor operations disabled immediately upon activation.
