IoT Hand Exoskeleton

Megha Mistry & Kevin Wang

Assistive Motion

Four precision servo motors provide controlled assistance to help patients bend their fingers, improving grip strength and dexterity for daily tasks.

EMG Control

MyoWare EMG sensors detect the user's muscle signals, allowing the device to respond to natural movement intentions and assist accordingly.

Precise Tracking

LSM6DSOX IMU sensors track finger movement angles with high precision, enabling accurate position feedback and recovery progress monitoring.

Remote Control

Wi-Fi connectivity through SAMW25 enables remote finger-by-finger precision control and real-time monitoring through a custom web interface.

Rehabilitation Assistant

Our hand exoskeleton improves user grip strength and finger dexterity, assisting stroke and neurological injury patients in regaining hand mobility. The device can be triggered either via EMG signal or remote Wi-Fi control, with an emergency stop button for safety.
