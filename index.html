Precision Motion

Four MG90S servos deliver 0–90° finger motion with 1° resolution and ≥20 lb grip.

Muscle Control

MyoWare EMG sensors detect your intent and drive real-time assistance.

Position Feedback

LSM6DSOX IMUs track each finger via I²C for closed-loop accuracy.

Cloud Sync

SAM W25 streams sensor data every second to a Node-RED dashboard over Wi-Fi.
